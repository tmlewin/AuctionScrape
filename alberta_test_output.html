REQUEST FOR PREQUALIFICATION OF GENERAL CONTRACTORS
The Town of Fairview (the Town) invites Prequalification Submissions from interested Contactors for the rehabilitation of the Towns Water Reservoir No 2. It is the intention of the Town to prequalify General Contractors with experience in the structural rehabilitation of water retaining structures and installation of approximately 100 m of 400 mm diameter PVC water pipe. The Prequalification is the first of a two-stage procurement process. This stage involves selecting the Respondents that meet the requirements of the Prequalification, who will then be invited by the Town to submit a quotation for the Invitational Tender to be issued on or before April 30, 2026. Submissions shall be submitted electronically via the Bids and Tenders website at http://ae-ab.bidsandtenders.ca and received until 2:00 p.m. (Mountain Standard Time), February 19, 2026. Prequalification Package Documents will be available on the Bids and Tenders website (https://aeab. bidsandtenders.ca) on or after January 30, 2026. Respondents are required to check for any updated information and addenda issued before the closing date and time at the Bids and Tenders website (https://aeab. bidsandtenders.ca). It is the sole responsibility of the Respondent to ensure that its submission is received by Bids and Tenders, by the stipulated time. Submissions received after the stipulated time will be rejected by the Town. The time as indicated by the declining time clock on the Online Bidding System shall be the official time for closing. Any changes to the Prequalification Package Documentation will be issued by means of written addenda and posted to the Bids and Tenders website and will form part of the Submission. No amendment of any kind to the Prequalification Package is effective unless it is posted in a formal written addendum. Upon submitting to this Prequalification Package, the Respondent will be deemed to have received notice of all addenda that have been posted and deemed to have considered the information for inclusion in the tender submitted. All questions related to this Prequalification are to be submitted in writing only, by email, to Sean Nicoll, P.Eng. at nicolls@ae.ca. Questions will be received up to 5:00 pm Mountain Standard Time on Friday, February 13, 2026. Questions received after this time will not be responded to. No questions are to be directed to the Town. PEACE RIVER COURTHOUSE Replace Humidification System
The intent of this Request for Proposals (RFP) is to solicit and receive formal offers from prospective Proponents for the provision of fixed price construction services for the Peace River Courthouse Replace Humidification System (Project). The project involves the demolition of the existing adiabatic humidification system at Air Handling Unit AS-1 and its replacement with an electric steam generator. The new system will include a steam dispersion grid and a water softener installation to provide humidification. As part of its Proposal, each Proponent must include a valid bid bond in the manner set out below: a) Provide bid security in the form of an electronic bid bond in an amount not less than 10% of the bid price. b) The bid bond shall be submitted as its own PDF document, separate from the other tender submission documents, in its original electronic format. Scanned or altered PDF copies of the original bid bond will be deemed invalid and may cause the bid to be rejected.
